Design Philosphy

There are many fonts to choose from my main goal however, was to find simple, minimalist fonts. I also wanted to website to have a modern look to it

After choosing which fonts to use I had to decide how many and which ones. Its not advisable to use too many fonts

I found that for this website it was necessary to have multiple fonts the content of the website isn’t enough to need more than two fonts.
