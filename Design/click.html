Design Philosphy

Clicking and Scrolling have the most to do with the layout of a given webpage. Should the content go on or should it be broken up into section?

This website doesn't have enough content to be able to use the scrolling method over the clicking method. Regards in the spirit of minimalism it is the perferred method.

Scrolling allows the user access to the most content is the most convenient way.
