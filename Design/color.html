Design Philosphy

Colors are very important in the design of a website. They accent and give focus to the content of the website.

Overall my main concern is keeping the website towards the minialistic side of the spectrum. To accomplish this and still maintain 5 colors overall each page has only one color.

Choosing the right colors is equally important. The best way to find your colors is by looking at a color wheel. Usually Analogous color pair well together and to get a focus color or main color Complementary works best.
