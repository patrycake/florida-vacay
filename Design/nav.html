Design Philosphy

There are a couple of options when it comes to navigation: fixed navigation bar, global navigation, Navigation with dropdown menus.

A fixed navigation bar has become a mainstream method for many websites however, it is important to look at the websites content before jumping on the band wagon. This website doesn't feature must content and a fixed navigation bar would bring more confusion then convenience.

Global navigation regards for content is always important. The user should never feel lost on your website. Along with that a user shouldn't have to voyage a confusion and unneccesary dropdown menu.
