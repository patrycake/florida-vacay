Design Philosphy

The website has a simple content that would do best with a minimalist design. This design is best suited for the users and gives the website a new and modern look.

To accomplish this I gave the website plenty of whitespace I added large margins, padding and space between text.

To create emphasis but maintain the minimalist look big bold texts with a little color and small texts are used throughout the website to focus the content but keep it simple.
